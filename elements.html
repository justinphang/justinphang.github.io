What Cookies Do

The purpose of acomputer cookie is to help the website keep track of the user's visits and activity. A website might also use cookies to keep a record of the user's most recent visits or to record their login information. Different types of cookies keep track of different activities. for example, Session cookies are used only when a person is actively navigating a website; once the user leaves the site, the session cookie disappears. Tracking cookies may be used to create long-term records of multiple visits to the same site. and Authentication cookies track whether a user is logged in, and if so, under what name.
